# Code of Conduct

AgentTestBench follows a simple contribution norm: be respectful, precise,
and oriented toward making the project safer and easier to use.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about defects.
- Keep reviews focused on the code, docs, tests, and user impact.
- Respect privacy when discussing logs, requests, responses, or Evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private data, credentials, or sensitive runtime Evidence.
- Repeatedly pushing off-topic arguments into project spaces.
- Dismissing safety, privacy, or maintainability concerns without discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to
temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges.
